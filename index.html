Oggy and the Cockroaches

Introduction

Oggy and the Cockroaches", the cockroaches are mischievous and troublemaking characters who constantly create chaos in the life of the show's main character, Oggy, a lazy blue cat. The cockroaches play the role of pulling pranks and ruining Oggy's peaceful, everyday routines.

Main Characters

Oggy

Oggy is the main character of the popular French animated series "Oggy and the Cockroaches". He is a blue, easygoing, and somewhat lazy cat who enjoys spending his time relaxing, watching TV, and living a peaceful life. However, his calm world is constantly disrupted by three pesky cockroaches—Joey, Dee Dee, and Marky—who love to cause chaos in his home.
